Hayeong Song

hsong300 [at] gatech.edu

{Data Science, Human-Computer Interaction, Data Visualization}

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Georgia Tech.

My advisor is Prof. John Stasko at the Information Interfaces Research Group. My research revolves around building intelligent systems that help users without a data science background to perform data analysis, understand, and train ML models. To do that I leverage machine learning models and draw upon techniques from mixed initiatives. Previously, I worked on combining multimodal (speech and touch) to visual interface to support data analysis with visualization. My research also focused on communicating uncertainty to users to help them make decisions.

I received my B.S. in Computer Science at the Handong Global University in 2016. I received my M.S. in Computer Science as a part of VisuaLab under Prof. Danielle Albers Szafir at the University of Colorado, Boulder in 2018.
